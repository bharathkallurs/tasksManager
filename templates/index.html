About IdeasPlatter

IdeasPlatter came as a concept to help exchange and work on startup ideas together. "Together we progress" is the driving factor for IdeasPlatter.

Ideas are not unique, the way of execution is. When right people come together to work on a common idea they had, a better product/service can be built in lesser time.

Other than already daunting problems in startup scene, another burden people end up battling is competition. IdeasPlatter, will serve to connect people with similar ideas.

An idea can be enhanced and moulded with people of similar interests contributing towards achieving a single goal, than getting stuck competing.
